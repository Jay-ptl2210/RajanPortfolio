Custom Sheet Metal Enclosures for CNC Machines

I led the design and development of a custom sheet metal enclosure for a high-precision CNC drilling machine, focused on enhancing both functionality and industrial aesthetics. The enclosure was engineered to ensure durability, safety, and ease of maintenance, while aligning with modern shop-floor design standards.

Using SolidWorks, I created detailed 3D models, assemblies, and production drawings, optimizing panel fitment, cutouts, and access points to support the machine's operation and servicing needs. The enclosure was manufactured with precision-cut and tightly sealed panels to ensure minimal leakage and maximum protection.

This solution significantly improved machine reliability, extended component lifespan, and reduced maintenance overhead. It also contributed to a cleaner, safer, and more visually cohesive work environment.

Key Features:

  • Precision-sealed panels to minimize oil and coolant leakage
  • Full enclosure shielding internal components from dust, oil, and debris
  • Enhanced operator safety through protective design and integrated access doors
  • Clean, streamlined industrial design for a professional shop-floor appearance
  • Modular construction for easier manufacturing and on-site assembly

Skills & Tools: SolidWorks, Sheet Metal Design, 3D Modeling, Industrial Enclosure Design, 2D Drafting, BOM Creation, Manufacturing Documentation
