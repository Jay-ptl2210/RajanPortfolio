3-Way H-Beam CNC Drilling Machine Design

I led the design of a high-performance 3-Way H-Beam CNC Drilling Machine, capable of drilling on three sides of an H-beam simultaneously—top flange, bottom flange, and web. This solution significantly improved production speed and efficiency in heavy steel fabrication.

Designed for large-scale operations, the machine supports beams up to 1500 mm (H) × 2000 mm (W) × 12000 mm (L). Using SolidWorks, I developed all 3D models, assemblies, and production drawings, ensuring precision, durability, and manufacturability.

The result was a reliable, easy-to-operate system that enhanced workflow and reduced machining time.

Key Features:

  • Simultaneous 3-side drilling for H-beams (top, bottom, and web)
  • Large working area: 1500 mm (H) × 2000 mm (W) × 12000 mm (L)
  • Fully automated CNC-controlled operations with high drilling accuracy
  • Designed for high-duty cycles and long operational life
  • Modular structure for easier assembly, maintenance, and customization

Skills & Tools: SolidWorks, 3D Modeling, Machine Design, GD&T, 2D Drafting, BOM Creation, Production Coordination
